Loot Drops
Loot drops are items or rewards that appear when an enemy is defeated or a container is opened. The items are often randomized based on rarity or drop rates.

  • Randomized item drops
  • Rarity and drop rates
  • Encourages replay and exploration
Examples: Diablo, Borderlands, Destiny
